New Apple smartphone iPhone 15

Release date in September 2023

For more than a decade, Apple has been pulling off its favorite trick at the autumn presentation shows actually last year's iPhone, but in a new color and with minor improvements. One of the most cool trump cards of the Cupertino developers is the former autonomy of the new iPhone with reduced battery capacity. However, this cannot continue indefinitely, since the time of work cannot be seriously increase only due to the energy efficiency of the processor, and Android smartphones with their 5000 mAh batteries can already work longer than the Pro Max version. Apple decided to take a serious step, but buyers have to put up with the inconvenience.

According to the data leaked to the Web, the battery capacity of the iPhone 15 will increase significantly. But get closer only two models will succeed in Android smartphones, among which there will be no iPhone 15 Pro.

iPhone with the most powerful battery

The closer the autumn presentation of Apple, the more rumors about news leaks to the web. It always happens in a planned way, and the data is almost always correct. The leak was discovered by 9to5mac on the ITHome website, where close to Foxconn source talks about the iPhone 15 battery. Here's how it works will increase compared to last year's model range.

  • iPhone 15: 3877 mAh (3279 mAh)
  • iPhone 15 Plus: 4912 mAh (4325 mAh)
  • iPhone 15 Pro: 3650 mAh (3200 mAh)
  • iPhone 15 Pro Max: 4852 mAh (4323 mAh)

As you can see, we are expecting a really serious increase in capacity. Much, of course, depends on the energy efficiency of the processor, but Apple has never had a problem with this. Here's what's out of it follows.

  • The largest increase in capacity will affect the base iPhone 15 - almost 18%. For comparison, in previous versions it varied in within 9%.
  • iPhone 15 Plus is only the second generation of a smartphone, but the capacity its battery life will increase by almost 14%. This figure is again higher than the Pro Max version, and it will also come close to iPad mini 6 battery (5124 mAh).
  • The iPhone 15 Pro battery is again the weakest in the line, though capacity and will increase by 14%. This is due to small body dimensions and a large camera unit, which in this year, according to rumors, will be replenished with a periscope.
  • The iPhone 15 Pro Max battery will grow by almost 11% relative to the previous model and is still slightly inferior Plus versions. However, autonomy will be higher due to the new 3nm A17 Bionic.

Interestingly, Apple does not advertise the battery capacity of iPhones even on their website, for example, as in the case of the iPhone 14 Plus, which at the presentation dubbed "the longest-playing smartphone" - have to wait for iFixit parsing to make sure indicators. At the same time, Android smartphone manufacturers always emphasize this, often lowering the capacitance to "Typical" 5000 mAh, although sometimes it is 5 percent higher.

What the iPhone 15 will look like

Earlier we already wrote about the external changes of the iPhone 15 models: these are and an enlarged screen with thin bezels, and a new key volume controls, as well as an updated camera unit. New The iPhone will receive not only an improved battery, but also an increased the minimum amount of storage - now it will be 256 GB, however, only in Pro-versions of smartphones.

Apparently, Apple thought that 128 GB in the iPhone 15 Pro is too much few in our time, although the previous storage upgrade was only 2 years ago and immediately for all models. Now the amount of memory is one difference between regular iPhone and Pro. True, this time prices will not remain the same - Tim Cook has already let slip that smart phones will become more expensive.

For now, the base iPhone 14 and iPhone 14 Pro have thickness of 7.85 mm, but if the firmware increase in size is associated with camera update, then other models have visible reasons for this No. In general, the smartphone industry has taken a wrong turn: for comparison, the thickness of the Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra is already 8.9 mm, and shockproof IIF150 Air1 Ultra - 9.55 mm!
